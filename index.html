Howdy! I am a fourth year PhD student in the Mechanical Engineering Department at the University of Texas at Austin. I specialize in Robotics and Dynamics Systems and Controls. I am interested in enabling personal service robots to perform complex manipulation tasks with efficiency and elegance. I work with Prof. Scott Niekum in the Personal Autonomous Robotics Lab on leveraging interaction dynamics for making decisions under uncertainty.
